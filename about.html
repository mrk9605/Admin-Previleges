Who we are

Toms Sales Corporation is a professionally managed company which was incepted in the year 1964 with a humble beginning to serve the industry and since has become one of the leading wholesale stockiest and supplier in all kinds of pipes, fittings, valves and other accessories. With our head office at KOZHIKODE and branches at Ernakulam and Calicut, we have engaged to cater Industrial, Chemical & Domestic Industries with the help of the robust development in the Industrial World. We have also successfully captured the plumbing, HVAC and Industrial market in all south India under prominent brands like JINDAL PIPES LIMITED, SUPREME INDUSTRIES LIMITED, LEADER VALVES LIMITED and ZOLOTO VALVES LIMITED.

Our newly built office and stockyard at Elamakkara, Kochi is an edifice to our quality policy, providing complete piping solutions under one roof with sufficient stocks to meet any kind of requirement of our customers.

We have been certified to the International standards of ISO: 9001:2008 by the premier certification body TUV SOUTH ASIA. Our focus has always been on customer satisfaction by providing quality products at affordable price and after sales service which helps us to serve our huge clientele based in Kerala & neighboring states. Our principals are;

  • M/s. JINDAL PIPES LIMITED.
  • M/s. MAHARASHTRA SEAMLESS LIMITED.
  • M/s. SUPREME INDUSTRIES LIMITED.
  • M/s. LEADER VALVES LIMITED.
  • M/s. ZOLOTO INDUSTRIES.
  • M/s. NETA VALVES PVT. LIMITED.
  • M/s. RACER VALVES LIMITED.

Toms Sales Corporation is also into Petroleum, logistics, constructions and investments.

We look forward to the continuous support from our client and channel partners and thank each and every one who has participated in our growth.
